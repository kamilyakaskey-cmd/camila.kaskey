A Builder at Heart.

I'm a mechanical engineering student at the University at Buffalo who likes to test ideas by building them — whether that's a 3D-printed rover tread, a MATLAB solver for tuning-fork physics, or a hamburger-flipping robot. I care about the full loop: design, prototype, test, and refine.

Through ASME at the University at Buffalo, I've helped design and validate a tracked rover drivetrain and run large-scale mechanical systems testing, writing up results in an 80+ page technical report. Outside of engineering projects, I plan events for the ASME chapter and previously led a pledge class of 13 students through four team projects.
